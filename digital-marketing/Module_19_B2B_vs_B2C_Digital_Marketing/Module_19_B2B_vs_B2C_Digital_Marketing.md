## 19.1 B2B Digital Marketing

### 19.1.1 B2B Marketing Fundamentals
- How B2B buying differs from B2C buying
- The B2B buying committee: decision makers, influencers, users, gatekeepers
- B2B sales cycles: length, complexity, multiple touchpoints
- B2B marketing objectives: lead generation, pipeline, revenue influence
- Marketing and sales alignment in B2B
- B2B buyer expectations in the digital age

### 19.1.2 B2B Content Marketing
- Thought leadership content: whitepapers, research reports, industry analysis
- Case studies and customer success stories
- Webinars and virtual events as lead generation
- Technical documentation and product comparisons
- Blog content strategy for B2B
- Gated vs. ungated content debate
- Content by funnel stage: awareness (ebooks), consideration (webinars), decision (demos, case studies)
- B2B podcasting strategy
- ROI-focused content and business case creation

### 19.1.3 B2B Social Media
- LinkedIn strategy for B2B: company page, employee advocacy, thought leadership
- LinkedIn Ads: Sponsored Content, Message Ads, Lead Gen Forms, Conversation Ads
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator for prospecting
- Twitter/X for B2B: industry conversations, thought leadership threads
- YouTube for B2B: product demos, tutorials, customer stories
- Reddit and niche community engagement
- B2B social media metrics and benchmarks

### 19.1.4 B2B SEO Strategy
- Long-tail keyword targeting for niche B2B topics
- Pillar page strategy for complex B2B topics
- Technical and industry-specific content
- Case study and use case SEO
- Product page SEO for B2B SaaS and services
- Competitor keyword analysis in B2B
- Building authority in specialized verticals

## 19.2 Account-Based Marketing (ABM)

### 19.2.1 ABM Strategy
- What is ABM: targeting specific accounts with personalized campaigns
- ABM tiers: one-to-one, one-to-few, one-to-many
- ABM vs. traditional lead generation
- Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) development
- Target account selection criteria and scoring
- ABM and sales alignment
- ABM program design and launch

### 19.2.2 ABM Tactics & Execution
- Personalized content for target accounts
- Account-specific landing pages and microsites
- Personalized email sequences for buying committees
- Targeted advertising: LinkedIn, programmatic, IP-based
- Direct mail in ABM campaigns
- Sales-led engagement with marketing air cover
- Event-based ABM: dinners, roundtables, conferences
- Intent data for ABM: Bombora, 6sense, G2, TrustRadius

### 19.2.3 ABM Technology Stack
- ABM platforms: Demandbase, 6sense, Terminus, RollWorks
- Intent data providers: Bombora, TechTarget, G2
- Personalization tools: Mutiny, Clearbit, Folloze
- Direct mail platforms: Sendoso, Reachdesk, PFL
- ABM measurement: account engagement scores, pipeline influence, deal velocity
- Integrating ABM with CRM and marketing automation

### 19.2.4 Measuring ABM Success
- Account engagement scoring
- Pipeline and revenue influenced by ABM
- Deal velocity improvement
- Win rate comparison: ABM vs. non-ABM accounts
- Account penetration: number of engaged contacts per account
- ROI calculation for ABM programs
- ABM reporting dashboards

## 19.3 B2B Lead Generation

### 19.3.1 Demand Generation vs. Lead Generation
- Demand gen: creating awareness and interest (ungated content, brand building)
- Lead gen: capturing contact information (gated content, forms, demos)
- The pendulum: when to gate vs. ungate
- Creating demand before capturing leads
- Demand gen metrics vs. lead gen metrics
- Balancing short-term leads with long-term demand

### 19.3.2 B2B Lead Generation Channels
- SEO and organic content
- PPC: Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Microsoft Ads
- Social media: LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube
- Email marketing and newsletters
- Webinars and virtual events
- Conferences and trade shows (with digital amplification)
- Referral programs and partnerships
- Community building and forums
- Product-led growth (PLG): free trials, freemium models
- Intent data-driven outreach

### 19.3.3 B2B Lead Qualification
- Lead qualification frameworks: BANT, MEDDIC, CHAMP, GPCTBA/C&I
- Marketing Qualified Lead (MQL) criteria
- Sales Qualified Lead (SQL) criteria
- Sales Accepted Lead (SAL) process
- Product Qualified Lead (PQL) for PLG companies
- Lead scoring for B2B (detailed in Module 16)
- Handoff process and SLAs
- Feedback loops between sales and marketing

## 19.4 B2C Digital Marketing Deep Dive

### 19.4.1 B2C Marketing Characteristics
- Shorter sales cycles and impulse purchases
- Emotional buying triggers and decision making
- Higher volume, lower individual deal value
- Brand awareness and recall importance
- Social proof and influencer impact
- Seasonal and trend-driven marketing
- Customer experience as competitive advantage

### 19.4.2 B2C Social Media Strategies
- Platform-first content creation
- Short-form video dominance (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
- Community building and brand advocacy
- User-generated content campaigns
- Social commerce and shoppable content
- Brand voice and personality on social
- Meme culture and trend participation
- Influencer partnerships for B2C

### 19.4.3 B2C E-commerce & Retail Marketing
- Product discovery and impulse purchase optimization
- Flash sales and limited-time promotions
- Seasonal marketing campaigns
- Loyalty and rewards programs
- Subscription and replenishment models
- Personalized product recommendations
- SMS and push notification strategies
- Retargeting for B2C: cart abandonment, browse abandonment

### 19.4.4 B2C Brand Building
- Brand identity: visual, verbal, experiential
- Brand storytelling for emotional connection
- Brand consistency across digital touchpoints
- Building brand communities
- Cause marketing and brand purpose
- Brand measurement: awareness, consideration, sentiment
- Brand campaigns vs. performance campaigns

- Patient/client testimonial guidelines
- Compliant social media strategies

### 19.5.4 Real Estate, Education, Hospitality, and Other Verticals

- Education marketing: enrollment funnels, content strategy
