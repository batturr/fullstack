# Module 13: E-commerce Marketing

---

## 13.1 E-commerce Foundations

### 13.1.1 E-commerce Business Models
- B2C (Business to Consumer): direct online retail
- B2B (Business to Business): wholesale, SaaS, marketplace
- D2C (Direct to Consumer): brand-owned stores, cutting out middlemen
- C2C (Consumer to Consumer): peer marketplaces (eBay, Poshmark)
- Subscription-based e-commerce: recurring revenue models
- Dropshipping: fulfillment without inventory
- Print-on-demand: custom products with zero inventory
- Digital products: courses, software, ebooks, templates
- Marketplace selling: Amazon, Etsy, eBay, Walmart

### 13.1.2 E-commerce Platform Selection
- **Shopify**: features, pricing tiers, app ecosystem, Shopify Plus for enterprise
- **WooCommerce**: WordPress integration, flexibility, plugin ecosystem
- **BigCommerce**: built-in features, B2B capabilities, headless commerce
- **Magento/Adobe Commerce**: enterprise, customization, complex catalogs
- **Squarespace Commerce**: design-focused, simple catalog
- **Wix eCommerce**: drag-and-drop, small catalogs
- **Headless commerce**: Shopify Hydrogen, commercetools, BigCommerce + Next.js
- Platform comparison criteria: scalability, cost, features, integrations, support

### 13.1.3 E-commerce Tech Stack
- Payment processors: Stripe, PayPal, Square, Klarna, Afterpay (BNPL)
- Shipping and fulfillment: ShipStation, ShipBob, Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA)
- Inventory management: TradeGecko, Cin7, Skubana
- Customer support: Zendesk, Gorgias, Freshdesk
- Reviews: Yotpo, Judge.me, Stamped, Trustpilot
- Loyalty: Smile.io, LoyaltyLion, Yotpo Loyalty
- Personalization: Nosto, Dynamic Yield, Rebuy
- Analytics: Triple Whale, Lifetimely, Google Analytics

- Product titles: keywords, specifics, brand + product + attributes
- Product descriptions: benefits-focused, scannable, SEO-optimized
- Product photography: main images, lifestyle shots, detail shots, 360-degree views
- Product videos: demonstrations, unboxings, how-to-use
- Pricing display: anchoring, crossed-out prices, bundle pricing
- Trust elements: reviews, ratings, security badges, return policy

- Cross-sell and upsell sections

### 13.2.2 Category Page Optimization
