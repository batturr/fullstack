# Module 18: Digital Marketing Strategy & Planning

---

## 18.1 Strategic Framework

### 18.1.1 Strategy vs. Tactics
- What makes a strategy (long-term direction and choices)
- What makes a tactic (specific actions within a strategy)
- The strategy hierarchy: business → marketing → digital → channel
- Common strategic mistakes: tactics without strategy, too many channels, no focus
- Thinking like a strategist: market positioning, differentiation, competitive advantage

### 18.1.2 Strategic Planning Frameworks
- SOSTAC framework: Situation, Objectives, Strategy, Tactics, Action, Control
- RACE framework: Reach, Act, Convert, Engage
- OGSM: Objectives, Goals, Strategies, Measures
- McKinsey 7S model applied to digital marketing
- Blue Ocean Strategy for digital differentiation
- Growth marketing framework: acquisition → activation → retention → referral → revenue

### 18.1.3 Market Positioning & Differentiation
- Positioning strategy development
- Competitive positioning maps
- Value proposition canvas
- Differentiation strategies: product, price, service, experience, brand, community
- Niche vs. mass market positioning
- Category creation and market definition
- Brand positioning statement template
