- Brand messaging: tagline, elevator pitch, bio variations

### 21.1.3 Visual Personal Branding
- Professional headshots and brand photography
- Personal logo and visual identity (colors, fonts, style)
- Consistent visual presence across platforms
- Personal website/portfolio design

- Video and audio branding elements

## 21.3 Thought Leadership Development

### 21.3.1 Becoming a Thought Leader
- What distinguishes thought leaders from content creators
- Developing unique perspectives and frameworks
- Identifying your thought leadership niche
- Original research and data as thought leadership fuel
- Contrarian viewpoints and challenging conventional wisdom
- Consistency and long-term commitment
- Building credibility through results and experience

### 21.3.2 Content Creation for Authority
- Long-form writing: in-depth articles, whitepapers, books
- Short-form content: social posts, threads, newsletters
- Video content: tutorials, analysis, commentary
- Podcast hosting and guesting
- Frameworks and methodologies (creating your own "system")
- Data-driven content and original research
- Case studies and transparent sharing of results
- Content cadence and consistency

### 21.3.3 Public Speaking & Events
- Finding speaking opportunities: conferences, meetups, webinars, podcasts
- CFP (Call for Proposals) writing
- Presentation design and delivery skills
- Keynote vs. workshop vs. panel vs. fireside chat preparation
- Virtual vs. in-person speaking strategies
- Building a speaker profile and one-sheet
- Speaker bureaus and management
- Leveraging speaking for content creation (talk → blog → social → newsletter)

### 21.3.4 Writing a Book or Course
- Why writing a book establishes authority
- Self-publishing vs. traditional publishing
- Book marketing and launch strategy
- Online course creation: platforms (Teachable, Thinkific, Kajabi, Maven)
- Course curriculum design and pricing
- Marketing your course with content and ads
- Membership communities around your expertise
