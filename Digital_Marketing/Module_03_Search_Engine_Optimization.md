- Other search engines: Bing, Yahoo, DuckDuckGo, Yandex, Baidu

### 3.1.2 Google's Algorithm History & Major Updates
- PageRank: the original algorithm concept
- Panda (2011): content quality
- Penguin (2012): link spam
- Hummingbird (2013): semantic search
- Mobilegeddon (2015): mobile-friendliness
- RankBrain (2015): machine learning
- BERT (2019): natural language understanding
- Core Web Vitals (2021): page experience
- Helpful Content Update (2022-2024): people-first content
- March 2024 Core Update: spam policies and site reputation abuse
- How to recover from algorithm penalties

### 3.1.3 Search Intent & Query Types
- Informational intent: "how to," "what is," "guide"
- Navigational intent: brand searches, specific site searches
- Commercial investigation: "best," "review," "comparison"
- Transactional intent: "buy," "price," "discount," "near me"
- Mapping content types to search intent
- Analyzing SERP features to understand intent

- YMYL (Your Money Your Life) pages and higher standards
- Building author authority and expertise signals
- Demonstrating real-world experience

- Practical steps to improve E-E-A-T across your site
