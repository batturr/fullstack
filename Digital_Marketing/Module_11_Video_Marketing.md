- **Webinars and live events**: interactive, Q&A, panel discussions
- **Social media videos**: Reels, TikToks, Stories, Shorts
- **Video ads**: pre-roll, mid-roll, sponsored content
- **User-generated video content**: unboxing, reviews, challenges
- **Recruitment and employer branding videos**
- **Event coverage and recap videos**
- **Animated videos and motion graphics**

## 11.2 Video Production

### 11.2.1 Pre-Production
- Concept development and ideation
- Scriptwriting: structure, pacing, hooks, CTAs
- Storyboarding and shot lists
- Talent casting: on-camera hosts, voiceover artists
- Location scouting and set design
- Props, wardrobe, and branding elements
- Production timeline and scheduling
- Budget planning and resource allocation

### 11.2.2 Production Equipment & Setup
- Cameras: smartphones, DSLRs, mirrorless, cinema cameras
- Lenses: wide, standard, telephoto for different content types
- Lighting: three-point lighting, ring lights, natural light techniques
- Audio: lavalier mics, shotgun mics, USB mics, audio recorders
- Tripods, gimbals, and stabilization
- Teleprompters and cue cards
- Green screens and virtual backgrounds
- Multi-camera setups
- Remote/virtual production setups

### 11.2.3 Filming Techniques
- Framing and composition rules (rule of thirds, leading lines, headroom)
- Camera angles and movements (pan, tilt, zoom, dolly)
- B-roll capture for visual interest
- Interview setup and technique
- Screen recording and screencasting (Loom, OBS, Camtasia)
- Smartphone filming best practices
- Talking head format optimization
- Product photography and videography

### 11.2.4 Post-Production & Editing
- Video editing software:
  - **Professional**: Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve
  - **Intermediate**: CapCut (desktop), iMovie, Filmora
  - **Quick/mobile**: CapCut, InShot, Splice, Adobe Premiere Rush
- Editing workflow: rough cut → fine cut → color → sound → export
- Color correction and grading
- Audio editing: levels, noise reduction, music, sound effects
- Motion graphics and titles (After Effects, Canva, CapCut)
- Subtitles and captions: auto-generated, SRT files, burnt-in
- Thumbnail creation for YouTube
- Export settings for different platforms (resolution, aspect ratio, bitrate)
- Music and stock footage sources (Epidemic Sound, Artlist, Pexels, Pixabay)
