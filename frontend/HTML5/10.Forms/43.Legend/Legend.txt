Legend
========
• <legend> tag is used to display a title for the fieldset. 
• <legend> tag is a paired tag. 
• <legend> tag can be used only inside the <fieldset>.

Syntax: <fieldset> <legend>title here</legend> Your textboxes here </fieldset>

Example: <fieldset> <legend>User details</legend> <input type=”text”><br> <input type=”text”> </fieldset>
