Bold(or)strong
    • <b> or <strong> tag is used to display the text in bold.
    • The text enclosed within the <b> tag will be display as bold.
    • Use <b> tag to display important text that you want to highlight.
    • It is a paired tag.
        Syntax:
            <b>bold text</b>
            <strong> text </strong> 
        Example:

            <b>Hello</b>
            <strong> text appear in bold </strong>
